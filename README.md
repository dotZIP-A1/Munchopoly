## Introduction

This is a simple HTML-based game created for the purpose of testing and expanding my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The current gameplay consists of clicking the juicy, amazing, mouth-watering food images on screen to increase your yums and continue progressing. Buy more upgrades with your score in the shop (bottom-right) to increase your yum score.
